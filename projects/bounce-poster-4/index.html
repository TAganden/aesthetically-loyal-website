Bounce Poster #4Nov/07

print, poster, photography, opulent, party, flyer, promotion, miniatures, diorama

Client

Opulent

Project Information

The fourth Bounce poster represented a major geographical and conceptual shift as the night was moving away from its original home at Third Class. To celebrate the occasion we created a miniature (1/250 scale) diorama based around the venue and its iconic naïve neon graffitied walls. The packed bags represented not only Bounce's departure, but also the propositional end of Third Class as one of Melbourne's premier club night venues.
